Willy Privacy Policy
Home Support
Effective April 9, 2026

Willy is designed to keep your guided affirmations private and simple.

Willy does not require an account, does not use ads, does not use third-party tracking, and keeps your affirmations data local to your device.

On-device features

  • Willy can read affirmations aloud using Apple's on-device speech voices.
  • If you choose to record your own affirmations, Willy requests microphone access only for that purpose.
  • Willy does not upload or remotely analyze your recordings in the current version.

Local storage

  • Willy stores your selected voice, questionnaire answers, recent affirmation history, and daily affirmation assignment locally on your iPhone.
  • Saved voice recordings, recording titles, and transcripts are stored locally on your iPhone.
  • Willy does not require account creation to use these features.

Widgets and data sharing

  • Willy's widget extension uses the shared app group only so the same daily affirmation can appear in both the app and widgets.
  • Willy does not sell personal information and does not share recordings with advertisers.
  • Willy does not require cloud account creation in the current version.

Your choices

  • You can use Willy without granting microphone access.
  • If you deny microphone access, Willy still lets you listen to and read affirmations.
  • You can remove local app data by deleting the app and its widget data from your device.

Safety

Willy is for encouragement and reflection only. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, therapy, or emergency assistance.
