Willy Privacy Policy
Home Support
Effective April 5, 2026

Willy is designed to keep guided breathing private and simple.

Willy does not require an account, does not use ads, does not use third-party tracking, and stores session history locally on your devices.

Health data

  • If you allow Health access, Willy may read recent heart rate and heart rate variability to recommend a breathing pace and session length.
  • On Apple Watch, Willy may also save the breathing workout you start so live heart-rate guidance can remain available during the session.
  • Health data is used only to support the breathing session experience you request and is not used for advertising or marketing.

Local storage

  • Willy stores completed session history locally so recent sessions can appear in history.
  • Recent session state may be stored locally so active sessions can resume quickly and widgets can reopen an active session.
  • Your latest watch snapshot may be stored locally on your devices to support paired recommendations.

Device-to-device sync

  • If you use Willy with both iPhone and Apple Watch, recent readings and completed session summaries may sync privately between your own devices to keep the experience consistent.
  • Willy does not sell personal information and does not share Health data with advertisers.
  • Willy does not require cloud account creation in the current version.

Your choices

  • You can deny Health access and still use Willy.
  • You can use manual breathing sessions without biometrics.
  • You can remove local app data by deleting the app and removing related Health permissions.

Safety

Willy provides guided breathing and relaxation support only. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, therapy, or emergency assistance.
