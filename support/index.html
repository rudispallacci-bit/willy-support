Support for Willy on iPhone and Apple Watch.

Willy is designed to be simple in stressful moments. If something is not working as expected, use the steps below first, then open a support request through the public issue tracker.

Open a support request Privacy Policy

Compatibility

  • Willy runs on iPhone with iOS 16.1 or later.
  • WillyWatch runs as a standalone Apple Watch app on watchOS 9.0 or later.
  • You can use Willy on iPhone only, Apple Watch only, or both together.

Common help

  • If Health access is denied, Willy still works with manual activation check-ins and manual sessions.
  • If you want biometric recommendations, allow heart rate and HRV access when prompted.
  • If widgets are not updating, remove and re-add the widget after opening Willy once.
  • If the watch is not reading live heart rate reliably, confirm the watch fits snugly and the workout starts cleanly.
  • Use the in-app Device Check screens on iPhone and Apple Watch to verify haptics and permissions.

Safety

  • Willy provides guided breathing and relaxation support only.
  • Willy is not medical advice, diagnosis, therapy, or emergency assistance.
  • If you may be experiencing a medical emergency, contact local emergency services right away.
