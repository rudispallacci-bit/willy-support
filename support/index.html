Support for Willy on iPhone.

Willy is designed to stay simple, calm, and private. If something is not working as expected, use the steps below first, then open a support request through the public issue tracker.

Open a support request Privacy Policy

Compatibility

  • Willy runs on iPhone with iOS 16.1 or later.
  • Willy is an iPhone-only app in version 1.0.
  • Willy includes Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets on supported iOS versions.

Common help

  • If spoken playback sounds different from your preferred voice, open Settings and choose another built-in voice.
  • If microphone access is denied, Willy still works for guided sessions and daily affirmations. Recording requires microphone access.
  • If widgets are not updating, remove and re-add the widget after opening Willy once.
  • If a recording does not save, confirm microphone permission is allowed and that the iPhone has available local storage.
  • If the daily affirmation does not match the widget, open Willy once and let the app refresh its shared state.
  • If Willy's guidance feels off, open Edit Preferences and retake your personalization answers.

Safety

  • Willy provides affirmations for encouragement and reflection only.
  • Willy is not medical advice, diagnosis, therapy, or emergency assistance.
  • If you may be experiencing a medical emergency, contact local emergency services right away.
