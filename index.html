Support and privacy for Willy, kept simple.

Willy is an iPhone affirmations app focused on guided sessions, daily widgets, and local recordings. This site hosts the public support and privacy pages used for App Store submission and customer support.

Support

Get help with guided sessions, recording, widgets, permissions, and common iPhone troubleshooting.

  • Theme, voice, and session-length guidance
  • Microphone permission help for local voice recordings
  • Widget and Lock Screen affirmation guidance
  • Troubleshooting for saved recordings, playback, and personalization

Privacy

Willy is designed to stay private and lightweight. Preferences, daily affirmation state, and saved recordings stay local on your device.

  • No ads
  • No third-party tracking
  • No account required in version 1.0
  • No remote collection of affirmation recordings in the current version

Willy is for encouragement and reflection only. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, therapy, or emergency assistance.
