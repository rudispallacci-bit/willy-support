Privacy

Willy is designed to stay private and lightweight. Session history stays on device, and Health access is used only to support the breathing experience you request.

  • No ads
  • No third-party tracking
  • No account required in version 1.0
  • No remote collection of Health data in the current version

Willy provides guided breathing and relaxation support only. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, therapy, or emergency assistance.
